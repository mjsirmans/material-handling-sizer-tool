Material Handling Sizer Tool

Engineering design calculators for material handling equipment

Select a calculator below. Design tools (Screw, Belt, Hopper) are standalone CEMA calculators — enter project inputs, review structural and performance outputs, export via print/PDF.

Screw Conveyor

CEMA 350 Rev 20 design calculator. Capacity, motor HP, torsional stress, bearing life, thermal expansion, shaft & spiral checks. Supports shafted, single shaftless, and dual shaftless configurations.

Live Open Calculator →

Belt Conveyor

CEMA belt conveyor design. Geometry, belt and material loading, tensions (T1/T2/Te), motor HP, idler bearing life, belt sag, vertical curves, and pulley shaft deflection.

Live Open Calculator →

Hopper

Live-bottom biosolids hopper design calculator. Volume check, LB screw sizing (capacity & speed), HP, shaft torsional ratings, and steel takeoff.

In Dev Open Calculator →

Equipment Pricing Tool

Manual project estimator. Enter equipment tags and costs, review GMaC/commission pricing tiers, and save project files. Pricing integration with calc tools is planned for a future phase.

Cost Library

Manage pricing data for all tools. View and update steel plate/sheet rates, structural angles, rubber belt pricing, purchased components (1,300+ parts), and labor rates. Upload Phoenix Metals steel quotes or Epicor part exports as CSV.
